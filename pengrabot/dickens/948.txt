Newton-Raphson Method is a root finding iterative algorithm for computing equations numerically. It helps to find best approximate solution to the square roots of a real valued function. Newton-Raphson Method is also called as  Newton's method or Newton's iteration. This online newton's method calculator helps to find the root of the expression from the given values using Newton's Iteration method.
