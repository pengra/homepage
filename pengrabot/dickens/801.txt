This wikiHow teaches you how to turn on your computer browser's JavaScript, which allows your browser to load and view certain elements--such as videos or animations--on some web pages. You can enable JavaScript using Chrome on desktop and Android, Safari on Mac and iPhone, Firefox on desktop, as well as Microsoft Edge and Internet Explorer on Windows. Doing so will resolve errors and pop-ups regarding JavaScript.
